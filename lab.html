Our Method

The Extraction Process

From raw botanicals to refined extracts, every step is precision-controlled for maximum potency and purity.

Step 01

Ethical Sourcing

We partner directly with sustainable farms in Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands, ensuring premium raw materials while supporting local communities.

Step 02

Cold Extraction

Our proprietary cold ethanol extraction preserves delicate alkaloids and compounds that heat-based methods destroy. Temperature never exceeds 40F.

Step 03

Multi-Stage Purification

Advanced filtration removes plant matter while retaining beneficial compounds. Each batch undergoes 5 purification cycles for optimal clarity.

Step 04

Lab Testing & Verification

Third-party lab testing verifies potency, purity, and safety. Every batch comes with a Certificate of Analysis.
