Infrastructure Services

Professional Botanical Infrastructure

Over 30 years of hands-on experience designing and building growing operations -- from backyard hobby setups to full-scale commercial facilities. We turn your vision into a working, efficient botanical operation.

What We Build

End-to-end infrastructure services for growers, processors, and botanical entrepreneurs at every scale.

Greenhouse Design & Build

Custom greenhouse construction tailored to your climate, crops, and budget. Climate control, ventilation, shade systems, and supplemental lighting -- from hobby hoop houses to commercial glass structures.

Irrigation System Design

Drip, flood, misting, and automated fertigation systems engineered for efficiency. Water-smart designs that reduce waste and optimize nutrient delivery at any scale, from raised beds to multi-acre operations.

Shipping Container Grow Rooms

Turnkey converted shipping containers with full environmental control, LED lighting, HVAC, and automation. Portable, stackable, and operational in weeks -- perfect for year-round growing anywhere.

Equipment Setup & Integration

Processing equipment installation, extraction setups, drying rooms, and curing facilities. We source, install, and integrate the right equipment for your workflow and budget.

Cultivation Consulting

Crop planning, soil and substrate programs, integrated pest management, and harvest optimization. Hands-on guidance from someone who has grown 350+ species across every climate and container type.

Processing Facility Design

Facility layout, workflow optimization, compliance planning, and equipment selection for extraction and processing operations. Designed for efficiency, safety, and scalability from day one.
